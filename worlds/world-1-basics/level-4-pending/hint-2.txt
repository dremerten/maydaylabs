🧭 Direction Hint:

The Events show something like "0/1 nodes are available: 1 Insufficient cpu" or "Insufficient memory".

This means the pod is requesting more resources than any node in your cluster can provide.

Check what resources the pod is requesting:
`kubectl get pod hungry-app -n k8squest -o yaml | grep -A 4 resources:`

The fix is to edit the pod with reasonable requests — something like 64Mi memory and 100m CPU.
Since pods are largely immutable, delete and recreate it:
`kubectl delete pod hungry-app -n k8squest`
Then apply a fixed manifest.
